One day, you wake up as a witch with the power to create sweets at will. You decide to make full use of your abilites, making a gingerbread house to live in and deciding to earn a living by selling sweets to the children in the nearby town. Soon, one is able to estimate how much a child of the town is pampered just by looking at how much weight the child has gained since the arrival of the witch--that's you! Now, once you are ready to jump into this life and see what awaits you, click 'Continue'!
